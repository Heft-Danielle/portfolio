Personal Statement

With over a decade of experience in education, I specialize in math, coding, STEAM, and technical subjects, igniting a passion for learning in my students.

I hold two master's degrees—one in Computer Science and another in Curriculum Development—reflecting my commitment to excellence in education. I’m particularly proud of winning an award for an applied coding curriculum I created as my thesis in my computer science degree. This experience fuels my passion for developing engaging, hands-on, project-based curricula that not only enhance critical thinking but also foster creativity and collaboration.

I believe that every learner has the potential to excel, and I strive to make complex concepts accessible and enjoyable. As a curriculum developer, I break complex technical subjects into manageable chunks, focusing on creating multiple entry points that motivate, empower, and engage. I’m successful in this because I understand the challenges that both facilitators and learners face.

Let’s connect and inspire the next generation of innovators together!
