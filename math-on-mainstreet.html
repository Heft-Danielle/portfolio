Math on Mainstreet

Northview Public Schools

Math on Mainstreet is a STEM-focused, project-based Algebra I curriculum designed to help students see the relevance of mathematics in everyday life and future careers. This innovative course bridges academic standards with practical applications, engaging students through real-world problem-solving and interdisciplinary projects that bring math to life.

  • Project-Based & Career-Relevant: Students apply algebraic concepts to real-world scenarios—like budgeting, architecture, and design—building critical thinking and math confidence.
  • AI-Enhanced Curriculum Design: Developed in collaboration with educators and generative AI tools to create dynamic, student-friendly content and materials.
  • Visual & Instructional Consistency: Implemented universal branding templates to streamline the learning experience and ensure clarity across units.
  • Cross-Disciplinary Alignment: Curriculum integrates STEM principles and aligns with Common Core State Standards for Mathematics (CCSS) and 21st-century skills.
  • Student-Centered Learning: Emphasizes exploration, collaboration, and relevance, supporting diverse learners through differentiated instruction and applied learning strategies.

This curriculum reflects my commitment to transforming traditional math instruction through cohesive visual and instructional branding that enhances clarity, engagement, and real-world connection for all learners.
